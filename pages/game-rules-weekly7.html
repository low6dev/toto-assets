5. The prize winners are determined in the following way:

  • The jackpot competition prize is € 5,000 unless otherwise stated. This price is awarded to the player who has the scores in ALL four Four Football correctly predicts matches in a relevant round.
  • When more than one player has the correct scores of all four Football matches correctly predicted the jackpot competition prize divided among the winners.
