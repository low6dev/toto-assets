1. Participants can predict 4 games per week within the premier league for a chance to win the € 5,000 prize pool.

2. Participants can give an extra prediction on one of the matches from a 2-2- as the final score. You have the chance to win an extra prize of EUR 5, -.

3. Predictions can be given and changed until the start of the first game of that round. Once the competition has started, the entered prediction can no longer be changed.

4. The prize winners are determined in the following way:

  • The jackpot is € 5,000 unless otherwise stated and this prize will be awarded to the player who correctly predicted all scores in that (concerning) round.
  • If several players have predicted the correct scores, the jackpot will be divided among all those winners.
  • If there is no winner, a draw will take place. A notary will take care of all people who have correctly predicted 3 out of 4 results, draw a winner.

5. When a Score4 match is postponed, abandoned or not completed (where less than 90 minutes is played), the game is considered as considered invalid. If only three Score4 matches in a round are completed, the jackpot for that round will be reduced from EUR 5,000 to EUR 2,000. If fewer than three Score4 matches have been completed, the match prize and consolation prize for that round are invalid.

6. Winners will receive an email to the email address used for the registration of the account. In addition, this is shown in the scoreboard in the app announced.
